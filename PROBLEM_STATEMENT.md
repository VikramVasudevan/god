### GOD - SIMULATION
This is a python based project to simulate this universe. 
On one side, there is birth, and on the other there is death.
There is natural resources available that are shared across all earthly beings.
In between, there is this thing called "life" 
the number of souls in this universe is a constant and they go through this cycle of birth and death, accumulate karma in the process, which causes more cyles of birth and death.
People are born with inherent qualities - Good/bad
People experience life in their own way.
The society has also created their own understanding of what is beautiful/ugly.
They have created race.
They have created faith.
They have their moments - happy/sad/the inbetween
So much dynamics in play.
Various events happen that impact each person in a different way that causes other events to unfold. 
People network with each other - make friends, find partners for their lives.
These seemingly random events that unfold - are they truly random - or are they part of a bigger engineering done by a supreme being. To keep this machine called universe running  in a self-sustaining mode. 
Curiously, what happens when there is bias towards good nature. what happens when there is bias towards bad nature? what is the impact to this universe in either case.?  will it cease to exist in either case?
this project aims to demonstrate this through a visualization. by letting us play God - atleast we would understand what it takes to be God :-)
